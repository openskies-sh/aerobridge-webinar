How can Aerobridge help you?

Know more about how this world-class open-source community can help your operations:


  • Standard way for your GCS and external parties to communicate with management server via APIs
  • Trusted flight module: Integration with ArduPilot and PX4 (coming soon)
  • Manage operational permissions and secure flight logs
  • Easily manage manufacturing, assembly and supply chain

Who should attend?

This is a webinar focused on the full "Aerobridge Stack" and the registered flight module integration.


  • Technical Developers
  • Manufacturers / Operators
  • Academic Researchers / R&D entities
  • Policy Makers
  • Open-Source Enthusiasts


What is Aerobridge?



Aerobridge is management server to help you with your drone flights and operations. With Aerobridge your GCS, Drones and Pilots interface with your company's digital infrastructure in a standardized fashion and be in compliance with the regulatory requirements around operating drones.
